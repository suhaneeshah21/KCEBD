Co-Curricular and Extra-Curricular Activities

At Kasturbai College of Education, we believe in holistic development of students. Our college promotes a vibrant culture of co-curricular and extra-curricular activities that complement the academic curriculum and foster overall personality development.

Sports Activities

Our sports program encourages students to participate in various athletic competitions. We organize inter-college tournaments and provide training facilities for:

  • Badminton
  • Volleyball
  • Cricket
  • Kabaddi
  • Table Tennis
  • Athletic Events

Cultural Programs

Cultural activities play a vital role in building a sense of community and preserving traditions. We organize:

  • Annual College Fest
  • Marathi Day Celebrations
  • Independence Day and Republic Day Events
  • Classical Music and Dance Performances
  • Literary Competitions
  • Drama and Theatre Workshops

Professional Development Programs

  • Skill Development Workshops
  • Personality Development Sessions
  • Communication Skills Training
  • Leadership and Team Building Activities
  • Subject-Related Seminars and Conferences
  • Research Paper Presentations

Social Outreach Programs

Students engage in community service and social awareness programs:

  • Health and Hygiene Awareness Camps
  • Environmental Conservation Initiatives
  • Literacy and Adult Education Programs
  • Women Empowerment Campaigns
  • Disaster Relief Activities

Academic Clubs and Groups

Students can participate in subject-focused clubs that promote learning and research:

  • Science and Mathematics Club
  • Social Science Forum
  • Language and Literature Club
  • Educational Technology Group
  • Research and Innovation Cell

Benefits of Participation

Participation in these activities helps students:

  • Develop leadership and communication skills
  • Build teamwork and collaboration abilities
  • Enhance self-confidence and personality
  • Promote social responsibility and citizenship
  • Create lasting friendships and networks
  • Balance academic and personal growth
