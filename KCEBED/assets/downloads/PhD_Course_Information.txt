===========================================================
Ph.D. (Education) Course Information
===========================================================

Affiliated to: P.A.H. Solapur University, Solapur

COURSE STRUCTURE:
- Duration: 3 years (minimum)
- Total Marks: 300 (Theory & Practical combined)
- Theory Course: 240 Marks
- Practicum Course: 60 Marks
- Viva-Voce Examination

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Must have passed M.Ed. or M.A. Education
- Minimum grade required: B+

CURRICULUM:

THEORY PAPERS (240 MARKS):
- Paper I: Research Methodology
- Paper II: Information and Computer Technology
- Paper III: Advance Knowledge in Core domain of Education

PRACTICAL WORK (60 MARKS):
- Research article publication
- Seminar presentations
- Field Visits
- Presentation of papers
- Literature review
- Research article review
- Participation in research festival
- Workshop participation
- Course work completion
- Discussion sessions on research topics
- Tutorial sessions
- Write up and thesis

RESEARCH SUPERVISION:
The institution is a recognized research centre of the University.
