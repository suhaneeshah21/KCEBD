===================================================================
M.A. Education Course Information (YCMOU)
===================================================================

Affiliated to: Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University, Nasik

COURSE STRUCTURE:
- Duration: 2 years
- Total Marks: 2400
- Mode: Distance Education

FIRST YEAR - COMPULSORY SUBJECTS:
- Paper I: Philosophical & Sociological Foundations of Education
- Paper II: Psychological Foundations of Education
- Paper III: Methodology of Educational Research & Application of Statistics
- Paper IV: Instructional System Design

SECOND YEAR - COMPULSORY SUBJECTS:
- Paper V: Communication in Education
- Paper VI: Educational Measurement & Evaluation

SECOND YEAR - OPTIONAL SUBJECTS (Choose One Set):

Option Set A:
- Paper VII: Educational Organization & Administration
- Paper VIII: Educational Planning

Option Set B:
- Paper VII: Adult & Continuous Education
- Paper VIII: Informal Education

PRACTICAL WORK:
- Dissertation & Viva-voce

ADMISSION DETAILS:
- Intake Capacity: 50 students

This program is designed for educators seeking advanced knowledge in Education through distance learning. The flexible format allows working professionals to pursue higher education while managing their careers.

PRACTICAL COMPONENTS:
- Dissertation writing
- Viva-voce examination

For admission inquiries and more details, contact the college admission office.
