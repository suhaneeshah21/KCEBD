Hello……! The Teacher Training Institutions are the backbones of the Quality Education System of the Nation. Every candidate has the dream of becoming ‘A Teacher with Nation building ability’, that requires an Institution which imbibes the rich–social and philosophical values, academic strength, innovative and creative applications etc. in its academic processes. Here is an opportunity for making all your above mentioned dreams true through the teacher education institution with the well-known name of ‘Kasturbai College of Education’, Solapur, Maharashtra. The Institute was established in the year 1970 to satisfy the thirst of ‘Teacher training requirements’ of the people in the region. It is managed by a very reputed Sanstha i.e. Shri. Aillak Pannalal Digamber Jain Pathashala, Solapur which was established in the year 1885 by the Hon’ble Late Seth Hirachand Nemchand the pioneer in Indian Industry and the institution was brought up by Hon’ble Late Seth Walchand Doshi and Seth Lalchand Doshi. The Pathashala’s Ex-Secretary Shriman Late Bhausaheb Gandhi has also contributed at great extent for the development of the institution. The Pathashala has been working on the Philosophy of “Shraman Sanskruti” with the motto “SHIKSHAN HACH DHARM”. At present the Institution is enhancing its wings under the able guidance of present President Hon’ble Shriman Arvindji R. Doshi and Hon’ble Secretary Shriman Dr. Ranjeetji H. Gandhi. The ‘Kasturbai College of Education’ has been rendering its Services to meet the social challenges in the region by keeping all these philosophical values and traditions as its base. The College with its specious area, well equipped norm based infrastructural facilities, creatively established educational–technology, psychology labs, advanced Language and computer lab with Internet facility, school based science labs and a rich library with about 25000 books and INFLIBNET facility, special sports ground with modern equipments along with highly dedicated, well qualified and experienced human resource which is ready to face every future challenge in producing the quality oriented teachers for the social uplitment and nation building. The institution for its resourcefulness, achievements and services rendered was assessed and accredited at B++ (CGPA 2.95) level by the NAAC. The Institution offers M.Ed. (Regular), B.Ed., DSM, M.A. (Edu.), and NET/SET, Ph.D. guidance Centre. It is very known ideal institution with rich academic traditions in the Maharashtra State. The Institution has achieved an extra-ordinary results in it’s all past academic years of all above mentioned courses. The outcome of institution is identified as proficient, efficient, industrious human resource in the educational area of the Nation. The Internationally reputed, highly conscious management with its rich technical, social, economic, scientific administrative experiences along with Jain Philosophical background doing every best in serving the society through educational contributions for the national development. In this respect it has extended its hand in every respect for the development of Kasturbai College of Education, Solapur. I have great honour to invite you to this glorious Teacher Education Institution to get Education and Training in our Institution for most respectable teacher profession.
