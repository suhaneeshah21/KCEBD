DR. RANJEET H. GANDHI

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to Kasturbai College of Education, Solapur — an institution that has stood as a beacon of quality teacher education for over five decades.

Our institution is managed by the Shri Aillak Pannalal Digambar Jain Pathashala, Solapur, which has been relentlessly pursuing the mission of providing quality education to society since 1885. Under the philosophy of "Shikshan Haach Dharm", we have consistently strived to produce competent, compassionate, and committed teachers who contribute meaningfully to the nation.

The management remains deeply committed to continuously upgrading the infrastructure, faculty development, and academic programs to align with the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020. We believe that a well-trained teacher is the cornerstone of any progressive society.

I invite aspiring teacher-educators to join our family and be part of this glorious legacy of excellence, integrity, and social responsibility.


DR. RANJEET H. GANDHI
Secretary, Shri Aillak Pannalal Digambar Jain Pathashala, Solapur
