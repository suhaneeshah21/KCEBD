Management

Kasturbai College of Education is managed by the Shri Aillak Pannalal Digambar Jain Pathashala, Solapur — a pioneering educational trust established in 1885. The trust has been a torchbearer of quality education in the Solapur region for over a century.

Governing Body

The governing body meets regularly to review academic progress, financial management, and strategic planning. It includes representatives from the management, faculty, and university nominees who collectively guide the institution towards its mission of providing quality teacher education.

  • Ensures compliance with NCTE, UGC, and university regulations
  • Oversees infrastructure development and resource allocation
  • Promotes research culture and faculty development initiatives
  • Monitors academic outcomes and student welfare programs
