Career Pathways for Teacher Educators

A degree from Kasturbai College of Education opens numerous career opportunities in the field of education and related sectors. Our graduates are well-equipped to pursue diverse career paths with their comprehensive training in pedagogy, subject knowledge, and contemporary educational practices.

Teaching Career Options

  • School Teacher: Secondary and Senior Secondary levels in government and private schools
  • College Lecturer: Higher education institutions and degree colleges
  • University Professor: Pursue research and teaching at university level
  • Online Educator: Teach through e-learning platforms and virtual classrooms
  • Special Education Teacher: Work with differently-abled students
  • International Schools: CBSE, IB, and Cambridge boards
