Ph. D. (Education)

(Affiliated to P. A. H. Solapur University, Solapur.) *This course is of Three Years (Minimum): * Eligibility: Should have Passed M.Ed. or M.A. Education with B+ This course is of Total 300 Marks including Theory & Practical: 1) Theory Course: 240 Marks
2) Practicum Course: 60 Marks
3) Viva-Voce

* The Curriculum of Ph.D. (Education) Course:

* Subjects:

Paper I – Research Methodology
Paper-II – Information and Computer Technology
Paper III – Advance Knowledge in Core domain of Education

Practical Work:

* Research article
* Seminar
* Field Visits
* Presentation of paper
* Doing review of research
* Doing review of literature
* Participation in research festival
* Participation in workshops
* Participation course work
* Discussion session on research topics
* Tutorial
* Write up
Ph. D. Intake capacity: As per vacant seats in research centre

Admissions & number of seats will be according to P.A.H. Solapur University, Solapur.

Institution is recognised research centre of the University.
