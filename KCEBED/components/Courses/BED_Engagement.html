B.Ed. - Engagement with the Field

The Engagement with the Field curricular area provides hands-on, practical experience in real educational settings. This area bridges the gap between theory and practice, enabling student-teachers to apply their learning in authentic classroom environments and develop professional competencies.

  • Skill Oriented Course (SOC)
