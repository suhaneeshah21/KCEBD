Information about DSM Course

(Affiliated to Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University, Nasik.)
The DSM Course is of one year:
* This course is of 800 Marks.
The Curriculum of Diploma in School Management:
1) Practicum Course (240 Marks) – * Assignment Writing – 140 marks. * Action Research & Viva-voce – 100 Marks.

Basic Subjects:.

Paper I – Educational Management – New Approach.
Paper-II – Educational Management.
Paper III – Official Educational Management.
Paper IV – Financial Educational Management.
Paper V – School Building & Physical facilities.
Paper VI – Students service, welfare and Scholarships.
Paper VII – Management of Human Relationship.

Practical Work:

* Action Research
* Assignment Writing: 140 Marks.
* Viva-voce: 100 Marks
* Theory Examination: 560 Marks
DSM Admission Intake Capacity: – 60.
