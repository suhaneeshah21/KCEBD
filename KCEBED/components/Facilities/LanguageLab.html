LIBRARY

The College has a spacious Library with a good number of book – collection. Our library is partially automated with LIBMAN Library Management System Software. There are more than 25,000 books, different periodicals, encyclopaedia, and journals etc. Collection also includes Diploma in School Management, M. Ed. (Regular), M. Phil., Ph. D., dissertations. We also have a reading room with capacity of sixty students which is open throughout the day.

The library is equipped with internet connection & sufficient number of computers and free access to internet for researchers & students.
